Our History

Bountiful Foods can look back on more than 80 years of history that made it the global company it is today. Everything began in 1938 in a place called Carlsbad in South Coast of California, with the production of fruits and fruit juices and the trade of dessert fruits from California Region. Today, the corporate headquarters of the group remains in this humble hometown so that it can continue to develop expert knowledge in the most important business areas: beverage compounds, flavours, and fruit juice.
